Lasagna

Description

Who doesn't love a good lasagna? They are perfect for family dinners, potlucks, parties, and they are DELICIOUS as leftovers. However, if you're like me, you have had your fair share of run-ins with BAD lasagna. You know, like the one that you grabbed in the buffet line that one time? Big mistake! Thankfully, making your own lasagna at home is a sure-fire way to ensure a great tasting meal that won't disappoint. Today, I will walk you through making your own delicious lasagna in under 2 hours!

This lasagna dish is sure you make everyone's mouth water the second that they smell it. We will be browning our own beef, creating a homemade pasta sauce, and using pre-packaged dry lasagna noodles for convenience. If you like to make your own pasta noodles from scratch, you are more than welcome to do so, and it will make your lasagna taste even better! This recipe is very simple. We will cook our pasta, brown our beef, create our sauce, and finally layer and bake our lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb 80% lean ground beef
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion (diced)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (red, green, or yellow) diced
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 (28 oz) can of high quality tomato sauce
  • 3 oz tomato paste
  • 1 (14 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2-8 tbsp sugar (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1/2 lb dry lasagna noodles (9 unbroken lasagna noodles)
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 lb mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 1/4 lb freshly grated parmesan cheese
